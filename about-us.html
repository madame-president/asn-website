About Us

ASN Finance Group was founded in 2021 by Tahir Ijaz, a CPA and Big 4 professional, who brings more than 15 years of experience. Our objective is to provide a high level of financial expertise to small and medium-sized business, which are normally available to large-scale businesses. We are a Vancouver-based advisory firm that brings in financial consultants and experts to offer staffing solutions to both corporate companies as well as to public practice firms.
