Step by Step

We've crafted a straightforward 6-step plan for seamless guidance. Our process guarantees efficiency as we collaborate to fulfill your accounting requirements.

Step 1: Demonstration

Discuss your requirements with the ASN team.

Share a copy of your accounting file with ASN team for analysis.

Step 2: Data Analysis

We will assign a dedicated CPA to analyze your accounting file.

We will share our proposal with different service options you may choose from.

You review the proposal for questions or adjustments.

You select your service and accept the agreement.

Step 3: Getting Ready

Our team initiates the process of setting up your file and making necessary preparations.

The implementation manager will make contact to schedule the initial onboarding call to get you started.

Step 4: Set In Motion

Schedule conference call with ASN's implementation team.

Discuss team roles and communication protocols.

Discuss specifics of key accounting processes including sales, AR, AP, inventory expenses, cash and payroll.

Step 5: System Set-Up

Develop client procedures manual (CPM).

Commence the daily workflow processing.

Step 6: Implementation

ASN team will get access to accounting software.

Organize the chart of accounts.

Walkthrough major accounts in the accounting system.

Establish view only access to bank accounts and credit cards accounts.

Secure access to payroll accounts.

Clear backlog for pending tasks from prior periods, if required.
